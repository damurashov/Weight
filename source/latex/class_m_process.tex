\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
M\+Processes are the \mbox{\hyperlink{class_m_a_s_s}{M\+A\+SS}} threads executing on various machines. They are responsible for maintaining some number of the total \mbox{\hyperlink{class_places}{Places}} being used by the entire \mbox{\hyperlink{class_m_a_s_s}{M\+A\+SS}} program, as well as the associated \mbox{\hyperlink{class_agents}{Agents}}. Each \mbox{\hyperlink{class_m_process}{M\+Process}} is referred to by its rank.
